# 📊 Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard
## 🔍 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Amazon sales data and generate meaningful business insights.

The dataset includes 2 years of sales data (Jan 2022 – Dec 2023), enabling trend analysis, performance comparison, and better decision-making.

The dashboard is divided into:

- __Executive Sales Overview__  
- __Product & Pricing Insights__

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing categories
- Analyze pricing and discount impact
- Understand regional contribution
- Monitor monthly trends

## 📌 Dashboard Features
__1️⃣ Executive Sales Overview__  
- __KPIs:__   
  - Total Revenue: 32.87M  
  - Total Orders: 50K  
  - Total Quantity Sold: 150K  
  - Average Selling Price: 219.15  
  - Revenue YoY %: 1.01  

- __Visuals:__
  - Monthly Revenue Trend  
  - Sales by Product Category  
  - Revenue by Rating Category  
  - Revenue Contribution by Region  

- __Filters:__
  - Product Category  
  - Payment Method  
  - Month-Year  
  - Customer Region  

__2️⃣ Product & Pricing Insights__  
- __KPIs:__
  - Average Rating: 3.00  
  - Weighted Rating: 2.99  
  - Total Discount Amount: 252.10bn  
  - High Discount Revenue: 9.48M

- __Visuals:__  
  - Sales vs Discount (Profitability Analysis)  
  - Price Distribution by Category  
  - Quantity by Payment Method  
  - Revenue by Product Category

- __Filters:__  
  - Customer Region  
  - Product Category  
  - Payment Method  

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- Power BI Desktop    
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)

## 🚀 Key Insights  
- Revenue remained stable with slight fluctuations across months  
- Fashion emerged as the top-performing category with the highest revenue contribution    
- Revenue contribution is evenly distributed across regions  
- Majority of revenue comes from lower-rated products, indicating scope for quality improvement
- Higher revenue categories have higher discounts, showing a direct relationship
